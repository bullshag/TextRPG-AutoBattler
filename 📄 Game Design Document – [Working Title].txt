🎮 Game Overview
You command a squad of 5 adventurers in real-time, text-based combat against enemy parties. Battles are automated — players win through team-building, stat optimization, and clever use of abilities and AI-driven tactics. Combat logs detail every event in a stylized terminal-style interface.
________________


🧩 Core Gameplay
🧙‍♂️ Character System
Each character has:
* Stats:

   * Strength – Affects melee damage & HP

   * Agility – Affects attack speed & cast speed

   * Intelligence – Affects magic damage & mana

   * Health / Mana – Resource pools

   * Attack Speed – Governs real-time action intervals

      * Abilities:

         * 1 auto-attack

         * 2–3 active skills with cooldowns

            * Tactic:

               * Determines AI behavior (e.g., healing allies, targeting lowest HP enemy)

                  * Passive Traits:

                     * Optional buffs (e.g., +10% damage if alone, immune to poison)

                        * Equipment: (Planned post-launch)

                           * Adds stat bonuses or passive effects

________________


👥 Party System
                              * Players manage a party of 5 characters

                              * Each character operates independently

                              * Team synergy matters — e.g., tank + healer + DPS roles

                              * Players configure:

                                 * Character slots

                                 * Tactical behavior

                                 * Ability loadout

________________


⚔️ Combat System – Real-Time Auto-Battler
🔄 Real-Time Independent Action System
                                    * No global initiative — every character acts on their own cooldown loop

                                    * Auto-attacks occur at set intervals (based on Attack Speed)

                                    * Abilities cast when their cooldowns expire and AI conditions are met

                                    * Combat is constantly resolving in a loop managed by the BattleManager

🧠 Tactical AI System
                                       * AI controls which skill to use, when, and on whom

                                       * Example tactics:

                                          * Aggressive: Always use offensive skills

                                          * Protector: Use shielding or taunts to guard allies

                                          * Healer: Heal lowest HP allies when under 50%

                                          * Debuffer: Target strongest enemy with control skills
